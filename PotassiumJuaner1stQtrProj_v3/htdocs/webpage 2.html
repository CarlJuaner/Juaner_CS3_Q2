Education: Those Uneducated

One of the problems that children more so adults faces as of now is the lack of education. This predicament is mainly caused by poverty wherein most people can't afford education for their children. Other factors also include the education system or failing schools, lack of education and education inequality. With the lack of education of these children or even adults, it may directly affect their life. Mainly, people learn to gain knowledge and they use these knowledge to pursue their careers in life such as to attain a degree.
